Dynamics (Turing Test)

Avg Transition Probability

Mean likelihood of generated trajectories based on the calibrated Markov Chain. Higher is more "human-like".

MW Test (Mann-Whitney U)

Statistical comparison of probability distributions. P-Value > 0.05 indicates indistinguishable behavior.

One-Step Prediction (Short-term)

RMSE (v, s, a)

Root Mean Square Error for Speed, Spacing, and Acceleration at the first predicted timestep (t=3.0s).

Open-Loop Prediction (Long-term)

Collision Rate

% of test pairs where follower collides with leader during the full prediction horizon.

minADE / minFDE

Minimum Average/Final Displacement Error over K stochastic samples (Geometric accuracy).
